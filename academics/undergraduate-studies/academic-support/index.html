Hopkins students have access to trusted academic and faculty advisers to help them pick courses and support their academic goals.

For those not sure what to study, we can offer guidance to make the decision easier. Or help with a change of majors. No matter what academic path our students choose, they have support to help them succeed.

The professors are very accessible. They’re wonderful about meeting you and helping you out when you need a hand.

Name
Annie
Class of
2016

Our faculty members are approachable and open to collaboration, and we have advising resources dedicated to counseling our undergraduate students (including those who are weighing a pre-med or pre-law track).

And as students start to think beyond Hopkins, they’ll have access to our Career Center for help finding internships and jobs. They can also tap into our network of more than 193,000 alumni living and working around the world.

Learn more: Office of Academic Support
